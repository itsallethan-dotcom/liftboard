# Forgeonix Branding Refinement

## Goal
Refine an existing logo and animation style into a professional tech brand identity.

## Assets
- logo.png = base logo
- frame1.png, frame2.png, frame3.png, frame4.png, frame5.png, frame6.png = animation references

## Keep
- Geometric "F" shape
- Angular, sharp design
- Assembly-style animation concept
- Cinematic feel

## Change
- Remove all metallic textures and 3D shading
- Remove purple neon color
- Replace with:
  - Primary: black / charcoal
  - Accent: amber/orange OR electric blue
- Reduce glow intensity significantly
- Remove gaming/esports aesthetic

## Style Direction
- Minimalist
- Professional
- Systems engineering / infrastructure feel
- Clean, modern tech brand

## Animation Direction
- Logo assembles smoothly (not flashy)
- Subtle energy pulse (like system booting)
- No excessive particles
- Final frame must be clean and usable as a static logo

## Deliverables
- Refined flat logo (SVG preferred)
- Dark and light versions
- Optional: simple web animation (CSS or Three.js)
